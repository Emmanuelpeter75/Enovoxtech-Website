Founder Story

E-NovoxTech was founded in June 2025 by Emmanuel Peter (Emmytoonz), a Nigerian tech builder driven by curiosity about artificial intelligence and a strong desire to use technology to solve real-world problems.

Starting with a simple vision and commitment to self-learning, Emmanuel began studying programming, data analysis, and machine learning. What started as a personal journey soon became a bigger purpose: building technology that empowers Africa and helping others grow through tech.

Guided by his Christian faith and values, he believes technology should not just be powerful, but also responsible, purposeful, and people-focused.

E-NovoxTech was created from this vision — to design practical AI systems, intelligent automation, and data-driven solutions that help businesses and individuals make better decisions.

Today, Emmanuel continues to grow E-NovoxTech step by step, focused on learning, building, and creating technology that serves both people and purpose.
