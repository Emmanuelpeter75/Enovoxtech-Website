Everything you need to know about E-NovoxTech — our mission, our technology, and how we are shaping the future of AI.

Company

E-NovoxTech is a technology company focused on building practical artificial intelligence and machine learning solutions that solve real-world problems.

E-NovoxTech was founded by Emmanuel Peter (Emmytoonz), a tech builder and entrepreneur dedicated to using data science to drive innovation in emerging markets.

We focus on "Applied AI"—meaning we don't just build theoretical models; we build practical tools that solve specific challenges like business inefficiency and manual data processing, with a primary focus on the African ecosystem.

Mission & Vision

Africa is home to the world's fastest-growing digital population, yet many infrastructure and business challenges remain. We believe AI can bridge these gaps by providing localized, impactful solutions that empower businesses across the continent.

To build a unified ecosystem of intelligent systems that empower Africa, serve the global market, and redefine innovation through responsible and purposeful AI.

Products & Technology

Our current research focuses on:

  • Sales & Predictive Analytics: Helping businesses forecast revenue.
  • Churn Prediction: Identifying at-risk customers before they leave.
  • Intelligent Automation: Reducing manual workload in administrative tasks.
  • Enovox AI: Our proprietary intelligent agent system.

We are currently in the Research & Development (R&D) phase. We are conducting private testing on several models. Join our waitlist to be notified when we move into public Beta.

Privacy is our core pillar. We implement strict data encryption and comply with global data protection standards to ensure that all information processed by our AI systems remains secure and confidential.
