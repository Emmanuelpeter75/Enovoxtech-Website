Predicting Customer Churn: A Data-Driven Approach

How we built a predictive model to identify at-risk customers and improve retention strategies.

The Challenge

Customer churn is one of the most expensive problems a business can face. Acquiring a new customer costs significantly more than retaining an existing one. The challenge was to move from a reactive strategy to a proactive one by identifying which customers were likely to leave before they actually did.

Our Approach

We utilized a robust dataset to train a predictive model. By analyzing historical behavioral patterns, usage frequency, and engagement metrics, we could identify the early warning signs of churn.

"By transitioning from reactive customer service to proactive data analysis, we can intercept churn before it impacts the bottom line."

The Tech Stack & Implementation

The core of this solution was built using Python. We handled data preprocessing and feature engineering to ensure the model received clean, highly relevant data.
