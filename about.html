Who We Are

Driven by Purpose. Powered by AI.

We are building the next generation of intelligent systems to solve real-world problems and empower the African tech ecosystem.

The Visionary Builder

E-NovoxTech was founded by Emmanuel Peter (Emmytoonz), a dynamic technical leader, Shopify developer, and aspiring data scientist. Driven by a deep passion for machine learning, the goal was simple: bridge the gap between complex algorithms and practical business solutions.

We do not just write code; we build scalable, cinematic, and highly efficient architectures. Whether it is predicting insurance claims, forecasting churn, or designing ultra-realistic front-end web environments, our focus is always on tangible impact and relentless growth.

Our Mission for Africa

The future of technology is global, but its deepest impact will be local. E-NovoxTech is positioned to be a catalyst for technological advancement across Africa.

By mentoring young developers, engineering smart automation tools, and deploying custom AI systems like the Enovox Intelligence Core, we are equipping businesses to scale faster, make data-driven decisions, and compete on the global stage.

Our Core Principles

Faith & Integrity

Built on strong Christian values, we believe in operating with absolute honesty, purpose, and a commitment to creating technology that serves and positively impacts lives.

Growth Mindset

In a rapidly evolving AI landscape, complacency is the enemy. We prioritize continuous learning, diligence, and the persistence to push beyond our current limits.

Applied Innovation

Theory is good, but execution is better. We focus on "Applied AI"—building practical, deployable systems using Python, Machine Learning, and seamless web integrations.
